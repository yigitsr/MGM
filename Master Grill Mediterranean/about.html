About Master Grill Mediterranean

Discover our passion for authentic Mediterranean cuisine

Our Story

Master Grill Mediterranean was founded with a simple yet profound vision: to bring the authentic flavors and warm hospitality of the Mediterranean to West Palm Beach. Our journey began when our founder, Chef Andreas, moved from Greece with generations of family recipes and a deep love for Mediterranean culinary traditions.

Since opening our doors, we've been committed to using only the freshest ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Every dish we serve is a celebration of Mediterranean culture, bringing together the best of Greek, Italian, Turkish, and Middle Eastern cuisines.

Located in the heart of West Palm Beach on Clematis Street, we've become a beloved gathering place for families, friends, and food enthusiasts who appreciate authentic Mediterranean dining.

Mediterranean Cuisine

Mediterranean cuisine is renowned worldwide for its health benefits, vibrant flavors, and emphasis on fresh, high-quality ingredients. Our menu celebrates this rich culinary tradition with dishes that feature:

  • Fresh Olive Oil: The cornerstone of Mediterranean cooking, used in our marinades, dressings, and cooking processes
  • Seasonal Vegetables: Locally sourced when possible, prepared to highlight their natural flavors
  • Fresh Seafood: Daily catches prepared with herbs, lemon, and traditional Mediterranean techniques
  • Aromatic Herbs: Fresh basil, oregano, thyme, and rosemary that define Mediterranean flavors
  • Quality Meats: Carefully selected lamb, beef, and poultry prepared on our signature grill

Every ingredient is chosen for its quality and authenticity, ensuring that each bite transports you to the sun-soaked shores of the Mediterranean.
